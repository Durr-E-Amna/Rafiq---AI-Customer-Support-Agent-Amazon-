# Return policy

Most items can be returned within 30 days of the delivery date for a refund or a replacement, as long as the item is in original or unused condition. The countdown starts the day the order is marked delivered, not the day it was placed.

Some product categories carry a shorter window. High-value electronics (laptops, tablets, smartphones, wearables) currently have a tightened 15-day return window rather than the standard 30, reflecting how quickly these items lose resale value once opened. Always treat the window shown on the specific order as the source of truth over this general guidance.

Customers do not need to return their entire order — they can return individual items from a multi-item order while keeping the rest.

## Items that cannot be returned

Some categories are excluded from returns for safety, hygiene, or practical reasons:
- Items that pose a health or safety risk once sold (e.g. some personal care and consumable items)
- Customized or personalized products made specifically for the customer
- Digital products and gift cards, once redeemed or delivered
- Items explicitly marked "Final Sale" on the product page at time of purchase

If a non-returnable or Final Sale item arrives damaged, defective, or materially different from what was ordered, that's handled differently — see the damaged/wrong item article, since the issue is on our end, not a standard change-of-mind return.

## How a return is requested

1. The customer opens their order history and selects the item
2. They choose a reason for the return (wrong item, damaged, doesn't match description, changed mind, etc.)
3. A return label or QR code is generated, and the item is dropped off at a partner location or scheduled for pickup
4. The returned item is inspected to confirm condition and completeness before a refund is issued

Returns are free at eligible drop-off locations for most items. Restocking fees can apply in two cases: up to 20% if an otherwise-fine item is returned after the standard window has closed (and customer service approves a late return anyway), and up to 50% if the item comes back damaged, used, or missing parts.

## Third-party sellers

Items sold and shipped by a third-party seller (rather than fulfilled directly) are required to honor the same 30-day return standard, though they may apply their own return shipping fees. If a third-party seller refuses a valid return or charges an unreasonable fee, that's covered by the A-to-Z Guarantee — escalate it rather than trying to resolve it through general policy, since it needs a claim filed on the customer's behalf.

## If a return is rejected

If the returned item doesn't meet the conditions above (used, missing parts, or outside the return window with no late exception granted), the return is rejected and the item is shipped back to the customer. No refund is issued in this case. If the item has a manufacturer warranty, the customer can still pursue that separately through the brand — that's independent of our return policy.
