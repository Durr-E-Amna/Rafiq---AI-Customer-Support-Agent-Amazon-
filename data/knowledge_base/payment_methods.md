# Payment methods

## Credit and debit cards

The most common payment method. Charged at checkout, refunded back to the same card if a refund is issued — credit card refunds typically take 3-5 business days, debit card refunds can take up to 10.

## Gift card balance

Stored balance tied to the customer's account, usable toward any order. Refunds for orders paid this way return as gift card balance within 2-3 hours of approval, since there's no external account to reverse a charge to. Gift card balance can also be the destination for a refund on a card-paid order in certain cases, but never the reverse — a refund never moves from gift card balance back onto a card.

## Stored payment balance / wallet-style payment

Some customers maintain a stored payment balance for faster checkout. This works similarly to gift card balance for refund purposes — refunds land back in the same balance, typically within a few hours of approval.

## Store credit cards

Co-branded credit cards tied to the platform sometimes offer extended financing or rewards on purchases. Disputes specifically about a co-branded card's billing, interest, or rewards terms should be escalated rather than answered from general order/refund policy, since those follow the card issuer's own terms, not standard order policy.

## A note on Cash on Delivery

This platform does not support cash on delivery — all orders require a payment method on file at checkout. If a customer asks about paying cash on delivery, that's worth clarifying directly rather than assuming they're asking about something we offer.
